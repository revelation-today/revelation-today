**Narrator:** *(this is the turn - let your voice rise with hope)* Before it gets any worse - something happens. Something no one expected.

God's people are sealed. Marked. Claimed, right in the middle of the storm, as unmistakably his own.

This is the army of God! *(let the people feel that this is good news)* And she fights like no army you have ever seen. Look for her swords - she has none. Look for her spears - she has none. She carries no borrowed violence at all.

What does she carry, then?

Worship that will not quit. And loyalty that outlasts everything the enemy can throw at her.

*(slow down here)* A great multitude, too many to count, from every nation, every tribe, every people, every language, standing before the throne and before the Lamb, wearing white, waving palm branches, calling out together with one voice.

Whatever comes next in this story - whatever storm still has to break - one thing is already settled, right now, before it even starts: you know whose side you are on.

Who sits on the throne?

**All respond:** The Lamb who was slain!

**Narrator:** Say it again, and let it become armor -

Who sits on the throne?

**All respond:** The Lamb who was slain!

**Narrator:** That is the whole army, right there. Not a sword between them. Just people who know whose side they are on, and will not let go of it.
