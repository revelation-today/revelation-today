This is Revelation told short - small enough to carry in your memory, not on paper. It is the same story, the same promises, just held down to what a person can keep close: God sees what is happening to you, this will not last forever, and staying faithful, even to death, is not defeat. It is victory already won.

Read it enough times to know it by heart. Then you will always have it with you, wherever you are.

**Production notes:** This version is deliberately short and made to be memorized, not stored - nothing here needs to exist on a phone or a page to still be yours. It does not try to decode every image in the book. It centers on the two witnesses and the martyrs under the altar, because that is where Revelation speaks most directly to a cost that is real, and a hope that outlasts it.
