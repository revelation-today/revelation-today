Jesus already won it at the cross, and the Church has kept faith with that victory ever since. On a sea of glass and fire, the faithful stand and sing. Their only qualification was staying loyal.

Not everyone gets to sing. Those who played it safe and sided with the beast to avoid the cost now face a far heavier one.

Two harvests: one gathers the faithful home, one is for judgment. Truth stops being theoretical. What looked, for a long time, like two paths with similar endings turns out to have led somewhere entirely different all along.
