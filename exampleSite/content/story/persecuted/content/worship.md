How do you get ready for what is coming? Not with a strategy. Jesus says: look up. Heaven's door stands open, always, onto a throne room where everything and everyone finds its true place before God.

Worship is not an easy fix. It costs something - laying your own crown down, giving God every part of your life. And in that room, one figure stands out: a Lamb, looking as if it had been slain.

He is the hero of the whole story - worthy, when no one else was found worthy, to open what needs opening and rule what needs ruling. Once you follow him through that door, there is no going back to who you were before. Whatever comes next, you belong to him first.
