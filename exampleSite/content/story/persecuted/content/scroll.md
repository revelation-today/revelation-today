The first strike has failed. Hard times can crack open just about anything - except a heart set on the wrong thing.

So a scroll appears, and John is told to eat it. It is sweet in the mouth and hard to digest, and something in it will change everything, though it will take time to settle.

This is not another disaster. It is a revelation - something true about God himself, breaking through where judgment alone could not. What people need is not more pressure. It is the truth about who God is, carried by people willing to hold onto it no matter what it costs.
