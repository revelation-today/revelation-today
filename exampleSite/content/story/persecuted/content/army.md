Before it gets any worse, something happens: God's people are sealed. Marked. Claimed, in the middle of the storm, as unmistakably his.

This is the army of God - though it fights like no army you have ever seen. No swords, no borrowed violence. Just worship that will not quit, and a loyalty that outlasts everything the enemy can throw at it.

You do not win this by being strong. You win it by belonging to the one who is - and by not letting go of him, whatever the cost.

Whatever comes next, one thing is already settled: you know whose you are.
