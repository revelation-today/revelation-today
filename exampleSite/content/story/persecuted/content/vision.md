John is alone on a rocky island, exiled there for refusing to bow to anyone but Jesus. A voice cracks behind him like a trumpet, and he turns to see him: eyes like fire, feet like glowing bronze, a face too bright to look at, a sharp sword coming from his mouth. John falls down like a dead man.

A hand lifts him. "Don't be afraid. I am the First and the Last, the Living One. I died, and look - I am alive forever. I hold the keys of death and the grave."

The kingdom you have been waiting for has already come, and you are part of it. Only one thing is asked of the Church: to shine - not on her own fuel, but held alight by the one who stands among his people.
