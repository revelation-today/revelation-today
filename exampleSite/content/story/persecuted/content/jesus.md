Now the same story is told again, from higher up. A woman appears in the sky, clothed with the sun, crying out in labor. A dragon waits in front of her, ready to devour her child the moment he is born.

The child is born anyway, and is snatched up safe to God's own throne before the dragon's jaws can close. War breaks out in heaven, and the dragon loses his place there for good - beaten by the blood of the Lamb, and by people who did not love their own lives enough to save them by escaping him. Furious, and running out of time, he turns his rage on the woman's children instead.

Take heart anyway: defeating the dragon was never your job. Jesus already did that, before it ever reached you.
