Two brave helpers told everyone about Jesus. They were not afraid.

Some bad guys tried to stop them, and for a little while, it looked like the bad guys had won.

But then — surprise! The two helpers stood right back up again!

That's how Jesus's story always goes: it looks hard for a little while, and then everything turns out right.

Jesus wins, and he loves us.
