Jesus wrote letters to his churches, like a teacher writing to her class.

To some he said, "You worked so hard! But don't forget to love me and each other."

To some he said, "You are poor, but you are so rich in my eyes."

To some he said, "Be brave, even when it's hard."

To some he said, "Wake up! I still love you."

Jesus knocked gently on every door. He never gave up on his friends, even when they made mistakes.

Jesus wins, and he loves us.
