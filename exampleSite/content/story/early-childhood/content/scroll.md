Hard times could not fix people's hearts. But something else could.

An angel gave John a little scroll and said, "Eat it." John ate it, and it tasted sweet in his mouth.

The scroll was a beautiful secret: God's story is true, and it comes out right in the end. That is sweeter than anything.

Jesus wins, and he loves us.
