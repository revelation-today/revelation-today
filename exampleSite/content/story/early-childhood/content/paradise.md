Then came the best part! A beautiful city came down from heaven, dressed up like a bride on her wedding day.

A big warm voice said, "Look! I will live with my people now. I will wipe away every tear. No more crying. No more owies. No more goodbyes, ever again."

The city sparkled with jewels, gold streets, and gates made of pearl. A clear river ran through the middle, with trees full of yummy fruit.

Everybody who loves Jesus gets to live there, safe and happy, forever and ever.

Jesus wins, and he loves us. Always and forever.
