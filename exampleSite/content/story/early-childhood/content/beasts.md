The dragon tried and tried, but he never won. He tried to hurt Jesus — and failed. He tried to stay in heaven — and got thrown out.

So he tried new tricks: two make-believe monsters, one strong and loud, one sneaky. They looked scary, but they were only pretend, copying the real King.

Pretend things never last. Only Jesus wins forever.

Jesus wins, and he loves us.
