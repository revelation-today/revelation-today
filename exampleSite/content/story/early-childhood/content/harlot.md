There was a pretend queen who dressed up fancy and sparkly. She tried to trick people into following her instead of Jesus.

But underneath, she wasn't beautiful at all. Her tricks did not work, and they fell apart.

Now the way was clear and ready for the real King and his people to be together at last.

Jesus wins, and he loves us.
