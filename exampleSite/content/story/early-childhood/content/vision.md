John loved Jesus. Some people sent him away to a lonely island because of it.

One Sunday, John heard a big voice, loud like a trumpet. He turned around — and there was Jesus! Jesus glowed bright, like the sun. John was so surprised, he fell down.

But Jesus was gentle. He put his hand on John and said, "Don't be afraid. I died, but look — I am alive forever!"

Jesus told John, "Tell my people to shine bright, like little lights, because I am with them."

Jesus wins, and he loves us.
