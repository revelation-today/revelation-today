John saw a beautiful mommy with a new baby. A big dragon wanted to try something scary — he wanted to hurt the baby.

But God swooped the baby right up to heaven, safe and sound! The dragon fought in heaven — and lost! He got thrown down, far away.

The dragon was angry, but he could not win. Jesus already beat him.

Jesus wins, and he loves us.
