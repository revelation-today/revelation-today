Before things got any harder, God did something wonderful. He put his mark on all his people, like a special sticker on each one, so everyone would know: "You are mine."

This is God's army. But it doesn't fight with swords. It fights by loving God and never letting go of him.

No matter what happens, God's people already know whose side they are on.

Jesus wins, and he loves us.
