Loud trumpets blew, one after another. The world shook. Things people trusted in, like food and money, stopped working the way they wanted.

You might think everyone would stop and ask, "What really matters?" But most people held on tight to the wrong things instead.

A hard day cannot change a heart all by itself. Only Jesus can really change a heart.

Jesus wins, and he loves us, no matter what.
