A scroll had seven seals, like seven locks. One by one, they opened.

Each time, something happened on earth. A pretend king tried to trick people. There was fighting. There was not enough food. It was a hard time.

But God's people who had died called out to him, and God said, "Wait just a little longer. I see you. I have not forgotten you."

Jesus wins, and he loves us.
