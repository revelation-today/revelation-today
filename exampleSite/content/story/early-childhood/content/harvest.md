Jesus already won the big win, long ago. Now his friends stood on a sea that sparkled like glass, and they sang and sang, because they had stayed close to him.

Some people had chosen the bad guy instead of Jesus. That was sad. But everyone who loved Jesus got to sing with him forever.

Jesus wins, and he loves us.
