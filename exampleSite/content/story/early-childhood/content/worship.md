John looked up — and heaven opened! He saw a beautiful room with a shining throne.

Everyone in the room was singing and worshiping God. Right in the middle stood a Lamb. This Lamb was Jesus!

Everyone sang because Jesus is the hero. He is the only one strong enough and good enough to make everything right.

Once you meet Jesus like this, you never want to look away.

Jesus wins, and he loves us.
