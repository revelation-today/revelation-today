Now the same story is told again, from further back - a different kind of Christmas story. A woman, clothed with the sun, is about to give birth. A great red dragon waits before her, hungry to devour her child the moment he is born.

The child is born, and rules all nations with an iron scepter - and before the dragon can reach him, he is caught up to God and to his throne.

The dragon fails. He is thrown down from heaven, and in his fury turns on the woman herself, and on all her other children.

We should not be surprised at what comes next: our task is not to defeat the devil ourselves - Jesus has already done that - but to draw people into a relationship with God, even while the dragon still rages, knowing his time is short.
