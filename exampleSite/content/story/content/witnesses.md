Now we see the material and the spiritual world as they truly are. The temple, God's presence among us, is not a place to earn favor - it is where we show our hearts by loving him, and staying loyal to him in this world.

The Church herself does not look convincing. She witnesses to the world with authority, but is constantly on the defensive, and is finally destroyed. The whole world celebrates.

And yet this is not the end. In her deepest, weakest moment, the Church is victorious. The world needs to see that even in suffering, we have a hope worth holding on to - and that they, too, can hold on to it. This is only possible when the Church walks as a faithful witness through hard times, resembling Jesus perfectly: authority on the run, and then risen, and lifted up.
