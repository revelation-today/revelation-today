Did you hear the news? It is true. The kingdom that we awaited is here! It has arrived, and we are kings and priests.

Before we celebrate this, there is an important task at hand, one that can not wait.

We see, in a breathtaking vision, what the purpose of the church is: to be the light of the world, while being fully dependent on Jesus. Nothing more is expected of the Church - but also nothing less.

The question is: is the Church ready for this?
