It is done, even if you never saw it happening. All along, while it looked like the Church was losing, she was ruling - just not with the kind of power anyone was watching for. That reign has shaped the outcome of everything.

Now comes the last reckoning: a final judgment, where it is decided, once and for all, who spends eternity with God - and who has chosen, all along, not to.
