Jesus breaks the seals to reveal that he is not the only one who intends to rule our world. Satan claims the same throne, and rides out first in shining armor.

But Jesus shows us what lies behind all of Satan's promises: violence, toward everyone who goes along with him. Conquest gives way to war, war to famine, and famine to death.

Already there is so much suffering in the world. The Church stands right in the middle of the chaos, and cries out to God. How long, O Lord?
