It is done, even if you never saw it happening. An angel comes down from heaven with the key of the abyss and a great chain, and binds the dragon, that ancient serpent, the devil and satan, for a thousand years, and throws him into the abyss and shuts and seals it over him - the same abyss that once had to be sealed to keep the deep itself in its place.

Thrones are set, and judgment is given to those seated on them, and the souls of those beheaded for the testimony of Jesus and for the word of God come to life and reign with Christ a thousand years - a first resurrection, though no one watching from below could have called it that. All along, while it looked as though the Church was only losing, she was ruling, in the same hidden way a valley of dry bones once had breath enter it before anyone could see it happen: I will put breath in you, and you will come to life.

When the thousand years end, the dragon is released for a little while, and goes out to deceive the nations at the four corners of the earth - Gog and Magog - to gather them for battle, their number like the sand of the seashore, the very phrase once used for a promise about a single man's descendants, turned here into the number of an army marching against them. They march up over the broad earth and surround the camp of the saints and the beloved city, and fire comes down from heaven and consumes them, exactly as fire once fell to settle exactly this kind of argument before.

The devil is thrown into the lake of fire and sulfur, where the beast and the false prophet already are, and they are tormented day and night forever and ever.

Then the great white throne, and the one seated on it, from whose presence earth and sky flee and no place is found for them. The dead, great and small, stand before the throne, and books are opened, and another book is opened, the book of life, and the dead are judged by what is written in the books, according to what they have done - the same court, the same opened books, once seen by a man in a vision on a riverbank, before he was told to shut up the words and seal the book until the time of the end. The time of the end has come. Death and Hades give up their dead and are themselves thrown into the lake of fire - the second death - and anyone whose name is not found written in the book of life is thrown in with them.
