It is done, even if you never saw it happening. All along, while it looked from the outside like God's people were losing - overlooked, overpowered, easy to dismiss - they were quietly ruling alongside him the whole time. Not with the kind of power anyone was watching for, no armies or thrones anyone could photograph, but real all the same, and lasting far longer than anything that looked more impressive. That quiet, hidden reign has shaped the outcome of everything, even the parts no one else noticed.

Now comes the last reckoning: a final judgment, and it is worth knowing this is not a frightening word here. It is settled gently, and it is settled truly - once and for all, without confusion, without anyone being overlooked or mistreated in the process. It is simply decided, honestly and for good, who spends eternity with God, and who has chosen, all along, not to. Nothing rushed. Nothing left uncertain. And then, at last, the way is finally clear for what comes next.
