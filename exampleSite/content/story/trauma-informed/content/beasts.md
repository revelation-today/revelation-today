Look at the dragon's scoreboard so far: it tried to kill Jesus, and failed. It tried to keep its place in heaven, and was thrown out. It tried to wipe out God's people, and failed again.

So it reaches for new tools - two beasts, one rising from the sea, one from the earth. One rules by force, one deceives with impressive tricks, and together they put on a grim imitation of God, Christ, and the Spirit, close enough to fool people who are not looking closely.

But look closer, and the imitation is already cracking. It conquers only by force, dazzles only by trickery, and builds nothing that will last - because the one it is copying has already won, permanently. Whatever power it seems to have, it is borrowed, and it is running out.
