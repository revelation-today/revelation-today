So it falls to you — feel it settle into your own chest right now — to get up and get ready for what is coming. But how do you prepare for something this big?

Jesus says: look up. Do it now, this instant. The answer is not a strategy — it is a throne room, and it is opening in front of you. Heaven splits wide, and there it is: the place where everyone and everything finds its true position before God. The door stands open, always, and it is standing open for you, right now, in this moment.

Do not mistake worship for an easy fix. It costs something — feel it cost you something. It means laying your own crown down, right there at the threshold, and giving God every part of your life, without exception, without holding anything back in your fist. And in that room, one figure stands out from all the rest, and your eyes go straight to him: a Lamb, standing as if it had been slain, alive and glowing with a wound that should have killed him.

He is the hero of this whole story — worthy, when no one else was found worthy, to open what needed opening and rule what needed ruling. The scroll is in his hand. The elders fall down. The living creatures cry out without stopping, and the sound of it moves through your own chest like a physical thing.

Follow him through that door — go on, take the step — and there is no going back to who you were before. You are not the same person who walked in.
