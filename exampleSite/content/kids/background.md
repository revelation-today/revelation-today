A long time ago there was a king who believed he was a god, and he ordered everyone to treat him that way. Everyone was supposed to worship him.

Of course, Christians couldn't do that, because we only worship one God: Jesus. That's exactly what the book of Revelation is about. And rulers like that didn't just exist back then - they still exist today.
