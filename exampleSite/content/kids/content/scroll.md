## Overview

Now it's time for the second mystery. The first mystery was the mystery of the church. We saw Jesus, who defeated everything on the cross, how the church shows up as an army, and finally how, through prayer, people's false securities get taken away.

But that didn't seem to work, because in the end people clung to their false securities even more. That's why we need the second mystery: the mystery of God. And that's what this is about.

And of course, it starts with Jesus again. We get to change the world together with him. We've seen that Jesus was worthy to open the scroll. And he has a (smaller) one for us too. And we're supposed to do something rather strange with it: eat it. What?

Well, that means we should make it part of ourselves - take it in completely - because it contains the mystery of God.

And here's the good news: by the end of the mystery of God, Satan's kingdom is completely destroyed.

And one more thing: no matter what happens, God is always in control, and he's watching out for us. Because what comes next gets pretty wild, and we couldn't handle it without Jesus.
