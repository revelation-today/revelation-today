## Overview

By now we've seen several examples of how a Christian should *not* behave. The beasts showed that very clearly. But we've also seen how much suffering that caused. So let's look at God's army again.

How do they respond? They sing a song full of joy, because Jesus is simply the winner. And do you know what their secret weapon is? Simply that they stay faithful to Jesus. Not great strength or cleverness - just trusting him and never denying him.

And now comes the big reckoning. Everyone who trusted the strong, mean dragon is about to face serious consequences, because they thought they'd get away with being mean, or with just going along with the crowd out of fear of being left out.

Now that that's settled, the great harvest comes. Actually, it's two harvests, because they're very different, and together they show what faith is really all about.

The first harvest is people being gathered to be with Jesus - maybe because they saw you being a good example and staying faithful to him.

But the others aren't just gathered - they're pressed like grapes. Their blood has to be spilled, because they thought they didn't need the blood of Jesus for themselves.
