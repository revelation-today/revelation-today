## Overview

Here we hear about two witnesses who show up and do a lot of things. But what actually is a witness? Witnesses are people who have experienced something so dramatic that they end up living differently from people who haven't experienced it.

You can witness a bike accident and start riding more carefully yourself, or you can witness someone stop being sad because they got a compliment, and you start giving compliments yourself, because you've seen what a difference it makes.

Here, the two witnesses are a picture of the church. The church finally shows up, and we expect big, powerful things to happen. And they do! They perform mighty miracles, and no one can defeat them. But at the same time, people are also afraid of them, and are glad when the witnesses are finally killed and mocked.

But then everything changes. In their weakest moment, when everyone is laughing at them, God stands by them - and suddenly everyone wants to get to know God. Why?

A lot of people spend their whole lives trying to never look weak or helpless, because they believe that if they do, everything is lost. But when they see us keep hoping, keep going, and see God help us in the end, they find hope again too. Not everyone - but a great many.

Would you like to be someone who gives people hope like that? Even if some people laugh at you and mock you for a while?
