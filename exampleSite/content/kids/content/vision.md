## Overview

Did you know that you rule the world?

No? Then this book is going to have some surprises for you.

We are kings and priests, and we have been for 2000 years already. And this book explains to us how we're supposed to rule. Because that's a very responsible job.

Jesus has already done everything necessary to make that possible. So what does he show John about how we're supposed to rule? It's quite simple:
- We should depend completely on Jesus, because without him we can't really rule. That means we should rule the way Jesus does.
- We should be light in this world. Many people live in the dark, maybe because they're afraid or ashamed, but we can bring them light.

We'll look at what that means for the seven churches next time. Is the church really ready for it?
