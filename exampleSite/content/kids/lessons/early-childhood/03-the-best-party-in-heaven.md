# The Best Party in Heaven

## The Picture

John got to peek through a door into heaven, and guess what he saw? A party! Not a party with
balloons and cake, but even better — everyone in heaven was gathered around God's throne,
singing to him together. There were creatures singing "Holy, holy, holy!" People bowing down
with joy. Everyone was happy at the very same time, singing to the very same God, forever and
ever.

It's the happiest, loudest, most joyful gathering there could ever be — and no one is left
out, and it never has to end.

## Our Refrain

**"Jesus wins, and he loves us."**

Try singing it to a simple made-up tune, the way heaven sings to God!

## Activity

**Singing Circle:** Stand in a circle holding hands. Sing a simple, familiar praise song (or
hum a happy tune together) while gently swaying or clapping, imagining you're joining the
party in heaven. Take turns letting each child say one thing they love about God before the
song starts again.

*Coloring option:* Color a picture of a big, joyful circle of people and animals around a
bright throne, everyone with their arms up or hands clapping.

## Simple Prayer

"God, thank you for being so good that everyone in heaven sings to you. I love you too. Amen."

## Note for the Teacher

Keep the "four living creatures" and "twenty-four elders" from Revelation 4 in the background
— you can mention "amazing creatures" and "wise elders" if a child asks, but the point of the
session is the feeling of joyful, non-stop, everybody-included worship, not cataloguing who is
in the room.
