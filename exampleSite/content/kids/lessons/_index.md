Full Sunday-school lesson plans for teaching Revelation to children, with an opening activity, the Bible story, an application, a memory verse, a prayer, and prep ideas (games, crafts, discussion questions, song suggestions).

Alongside the core elementary series, this section includes lessons adapted for early childhood, teens, and a range of specific audiences — regional (Latin American, African, Asian, Middle Eastern) and confessional (Catholic, Orthodox, Pentecostal/Charismatic, Mainline, Liberal/Progressive). Matching full sermon manuscripts for adult audiences live under [Sermons]({{< relref "sermons" >}}).

Unlike the short reading pages above, these lesson plans are for **preparing and leading** a group, not for quiet personal reading — each session runs about 15 minutes.
