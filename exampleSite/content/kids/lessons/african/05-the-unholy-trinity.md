## Copycats

Have you ever seen someone copy another person, trying to look just like them, but getting it wrong somehow? Maybe they wear the same clothes but act totally different?

In Revelation 13, the devil tries to do exactly that. He builds his own team of three — a copycat version of God the Father, Jesus the Son, and the Holy Spirit. It's called an "unholy trinity," and it's meant to trick people.

## Meet the copycat team

First is the dragon — that's Satan himself. Second is a beast that comes out of the sea — this stands for a powerful, prideful ruler who wants everyone to worship him instead of God. Third is a beast that comes out of the earth — this one looks gentle, like a lamb, but "speaks like a dragon." It tricks people into following the first beast.

Watch how they copy God: the dragon gives the first beast power, like the Father gives Jesus power. The first beast seems to die and come back to life, like Jesus really died and really rose again. The second beast does amazing tricks and wonders, like the Holy Spirit works through God's people.

## How to tell the difference

From far away, the copycat team might even look a little like the real thing. So how do you tell them apart? Listen to the voice. The second beast "speaks like a dragon" — even though it looks gentle. That's the giveaway.

Jesus said his true followers know his voice (John 10:1-5). And here's the biggest difference of all: the copycat team gets what it wants through scaring people and forcing people. God never does that. God only works through love.

## A note about spiritual things

You might hear grown-ups talk about being careful of "bad spirits" or "spiritual attacks" — that's a real and important thing! This chapter helps us know exactly what to watch for: anything that tries to scare us or force us into following it isn't from God, no matter how powerful or impressive it looks.

## Talk about it

Have you ever seen someone try to get their way by scaring others, instead of asking nicely? How is that different from how God treats us? How can you tell if something is really from God, or just looks powerful but isn't?

**Memory verse:** "My sheep listen to my voice; I know them, and they follow me." (John 10:27)

## Prayer

Dear God, help us to know your voice, even when something else looks powerful or exciting. Help us to remember that you never force us or scare us — you only love us. Amen.
