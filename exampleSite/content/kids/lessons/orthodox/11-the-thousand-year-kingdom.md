# Reigning Right Now
### A kingdom that's already happening

---

## 1. Opening: When does it start?

*(Ask kids: "If someone told you a party starts 'after school,' does that mean right after the bell, or maybe way later? How do you know?")*

Sometimes one little word can be confusing. In Revelation chapter 20, there's a "thousand-year kingdom," and people have argued for a long, long time about exactly when it happens. Today we'll see that the answer might be simpler — and more exciting — than people think.

---

## 2. Already reigning?

Revelation 20 talks about people who died for loving Jesus, and says they "came to life and reigned with Christ for a thousand years." Some people think this means a special future time still coming. But looking closely at the Bible, it seems like this "thousand years" describes something that's already going on — right now! — ever since Jesus rose from the dead.

---

## 3. Jesus already won

Remember: Jesus already defeated Satan on the cross and when he rose again! The Bible says Satan is already "bound," meaning his power is already broken (Mark 3:27) — even though the world still has hard and scary things happening in it sometimes. It's a bit like when a big scary storm is already losing its power, but there are still some strong winds left before it's completely over.

---

## 4. Dying for Jesus is like reigning like a king

Here's something amazing: the Bible says people who die because they love Jesus are like kings, reigning with him — right away, not waiting for later! This is why our Church remembers and honors martyrs (people who died for their faith) so much. They're not waiting around for their reward — in a real sense, they're already ruling with Jesus in glory.

---

## 5. So what does this mean for us?

It means we don't have to wait for "someday" to start living like citizens of God's kingdom. Every time we choose to be faithful — even in small ways, like being kind when it's hard, or standing up for what's right — we're living out that same kind of reigning, right now, with Jesus.

---

## 6. Memory verse

"They came to life and reigned with Christ for a thousand years." *(Revelation 20:4)*

---

## 7. Prayer

Dear Jesus, thank you that you already won the victory over Satan. Help us to live like citizens of your kingdom right now, not just someday. Amen.

---

## Appendix: Ideas for preparation

**Opening idea:** The "when does the party start" word game described above helps kids grasp the ambiguity of "and" language before diving into the passage.

**Craft idea:** Have kids make a small paper crown and write on it one way they can "reign" (live faithfully) this week — kindness, honesty, courage.

**Questions for the kids:**
1. What does it mean that Jesus already won, even though the world still has hard things in it?
2. Why do you think our Church honors martyrs so much?
3. What's one way you can "reign with Jesus" this week, even in something small?

**Song suggestions:**
- A children's song about Jesus's victory or resurrection
- A simple song about the Kingdom of God
