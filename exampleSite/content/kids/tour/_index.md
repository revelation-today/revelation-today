Welcome! Have you ever heard grown-ups say that the last book of the Bible isn't for kids - that it's too strange, too scary, even for adults to read?

That's not true at all. It's actually one of the most exciting books in the whole Bible, and it has a lot to tell you. Come with me on this tour.
