God's army fights like no army you've seen — no swords, no borrowed violence, just relentless worship and a loyalty that outlasts whatever the enemy throws at it. Before anything else happens, one thing is already settled: you're marked as his, claimed in the middle of the storm, not waiting to earn a place afterward.

That security is meant to change how you fight, not just reassure you that you're safe. Worship and loyalty are the weapons here, not because they're gentler, but because they're the only ones that actually work against this enemy.

- If your only weapons this week were worship and loyalty rather than control, worry, or force, what would you actually do differently in the situation that's pressing on you most?
- Whose side are you on when nobody's watching, not just when it's easy to say out loud?
