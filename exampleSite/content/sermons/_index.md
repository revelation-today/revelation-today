This is Revelation prepared to preach and teach: full sermon manuscripts and small-group guides built on the same explanations and applications found elsewhere on this site, adapted for the specific audience using them.

Alongside the core sermon series, this section includes adaptation packs for specific audiences — regional (Latin American, African, Asian, Middle Eastern), confessional (Catholic, Orthodox, Pentecostal/Charismatic, Mainline, Liberal/Progressive), and a short module directly addressing prosperity-gospel teaching. Each pack affirms the real strengths of that audience's tradition, addresses real tensions constructively, and names the challenges that audience faces so others can understand it too. Matching kids' Sunday-school lesson plans for each of these audiences live under [Kids → Lessons]({{< relref "kids/lessons" >}}).

**Best for:** pastors and small-group leaders preparing to teach Revelation to a specific congregation or audience.
